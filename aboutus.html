Our Story

Founded in 2012 by Sarah Wellington, Serene Beauty began as a small studio with a big vision: to create a sanctuary where beauty treatments transcend the ordinary and become transformative experiences.

Sarah believed that true beauty emerges when one feels nurtured, respected, and understood. What started as a single-treatment room has blossomed into a premier beauty destination, serving thousands of clients with our signature blend of luxury, expertise, and genuine care.

"Beauty is not just about appearance; it's about how you feel. Our mission is to create spaces where every individual can discover their unique radiance."

Sarah Wellington
Founder & Creative Director
