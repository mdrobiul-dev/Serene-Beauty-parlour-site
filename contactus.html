Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and policies.

What is your cancellation policy?
We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Appointments canceled with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the service price. No-shows will be charged 100% of the service price.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for weekend appointments and 1-2 weeks for weekday appointments. For special occasions or multiple services, we suggest booking 4-6 weeks ahead to secure your preferred time slot.
Do you accept walk-ins?
While we primarily operate by appointment to ensure the best service for our clients, we do accommodate walk-ins based on availability. We recommend calling ahead to check same-day availability or booking online to guarantee your preferred time.
What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, cash, and mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay). We also offer gift cards that can be purchased online or in-salon.
Do you offer services for special occasions?
Yes! We specialize in bridal packages, prom preparations, and other special occasion services. Our team will work with you to create a customized beauty plan for your big day. Contact us at least 6-8 weeks before your event for a consultation.
